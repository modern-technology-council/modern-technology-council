Technology Councils

The Modern Technology (MT) Council is just one technology initiative among many in Massachusetts. It is my intention and goal for the MT Council to work with all technology-related initiatives in the state. The core philosophy of Modern Technology, as Tim Berners-Lee (founder of the Web) envisioned it, is "Open Standards," and I am calling on all technology leaders to move toward this same goal, starting with the technology leaders of Massachusetts.

As collaboration efforts progress, we will announce other technology leaders who subscribe to the Modern Technology philosophy.

If you are interested in joining this collaboration effort, please use our RSVP form.

Andrew Faria - Founder and CEO iMedia Solutions, LLC
